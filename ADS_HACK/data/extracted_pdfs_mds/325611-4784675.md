## **Psoriatic Arthritis (PsA)** 

1. The member is 18 years of age or older; And 

2. The requested medication is prescribed by  or in consultation with a dermatologist or rheumatologist; **AND** 

3. ONE of the following: 

   - a. An inadequate response or adverse reaction to at least a 3 month trial of ONE non-biologic disease modifying antirheumatic drug (DMARD) or contraindication to ALL nonbiologic DMARDs; _**OR**_ 

   - b. An inadequate response or adverse reaction to at least a 3 month trial of ONE biologic DMARD that is FDA-approved for psoriatic arthritis; _**AND**_ 

4. Documentation of ONE of the following: 

   - a. An inadequate response (to at least 3 months of treatment) or adverse reaction to ONE of the following PDL preferred agents: Enbrel, a preferred adalimumab biosimilar product (adalimumab-aacf, adalimumab-adaz, adalimumab-adbm, adalimumab-fjkp, adalimumab-ryvk) OR Humira, infliximab (generic for Remicade), Otezla, Rinvoq, Taltz, or Xeljanz;; **OR** 

   - b. A contraindication to ALL of the following: Enbrel, adalimumab, infliximab, Otezla, Rinvoq, Taltz, AND Xeljanz; **AND** 

5. Documentation that the requested dose will not exceed FDAapproved dosing (see Appendix A).
