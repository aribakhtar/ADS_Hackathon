## **AUTOIMMUNE (AI) PREFERRED DRUG PROGRAM** 

**Line(s) of Business:** HMO; PPO 

**Original Effective Date:** 10/01/2020 **Current Effective Date:** ~~06/07/2025TBD10-1-251~~ 0/01/2025 

## **POLICY** 

This policy informs prescribers of preferred products and provides an exception process for non-preferred products. All products require prior authorization. 

## **I. PLAN DESIGN SUMMARY** 

This program applies to the disease-modifying antirheumatic drug (DMARD) products specified in this policy. Coverage for non-preferred products is provided based on clinical circumstances that would exclude the use of the preferred product and may be based on previous use of a product. The coverage review process will ascertain situations where a clinical exception can be made. For Humira, ~~and n~~ on-preferred Humira biosimilars, Remicade, non-preferred Remicade biosimilars, ~~Stelara a~~ nd non-preferred Stelara biosimilars this program applies to adult members who are 18 years and older requesting treatment with a non-preferred product. For all other drugs, this program applies to adult members who are 18 years and older and are new to treatment with a non-preferred product for the first time. 

## **II. EXCEPTION CRITERIA** 

This program applies to members requesting treatment for an indication that is FDA-approved for the preferred and non-preferred products. Drug-specific PA criteria will apply for all preferred and non-preferred products.  Newto-market products will be considered non-preferred until they are added to this policy.
