|**Background:**|•<br>**Kevzara, Tocilizumab**<br>`o`<br>Kevzara and tocilizumab are recombinant humanized interleukin-6 (**IL-6**)<br>receptor inhibitors that work to inhibit IL-6 mediated actions at soluble<br>and membrane bound IL-6 receptors. Inhibiting the signaling pathway<br>can lead to inhibition of activated T- and B-cells, lymphocytes,<br>monocytes, and fibroblasts. ## **A. Approval Criteria** 

## **1. Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)** 

When a benefit, coverage of an adalimumab product may be approved when all of the following criteria are met **(a. through d.)** : 

- **a.** The member is 18 years of age or older. 

- **b.** The member has a diagnosis of moderate to severe RA. 

- **c.** The member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to at least one (1) nonbiologic DMARD (e.g., methotrexate, leflunomide, sulfasalazine, cyclosporine), or all nonbiologic DMARDs are contraindicated. 

- **d.** If the request is for a non-preferred adalimumab product, the member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to two (2) preferred adalimumab products. 

## **2. Ankylosing Spondylitis (AS)** 

When a benefit, coverage of an adalimumab product may be approved when all of the following criteria are met **(a. through d.)** : 

- **a.** The member is 18 years of age or older. 

- **b.** The member has a diagnosis of AS. 

- **c.** The member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to at least one (1) nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID), or all NSAIDs are contraindicated. 

- **d.** If the request is for a non-preferred adalimumab product, the member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to two (2) preferred adalimumab products. 

## **3. Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis (JIA)** 

When a benefit, coverage of an adalimumab product may be approved when all of the following criteria are met **(a. through d.)** : 

- **a.** The member is 2 years of age or older. 

- **b.** The member has a diagnosis of moderate to severe JIA. 

- **c.** The member meets one (1) of the following criteria **(i. or ii.)** : 

   - **i.** The member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to at least one (1) non-biologic DMARD (e.g., methotrexate, leflunomide, sulfasalazine, cyclosporine), or all non-biologic DMARDs are contraindicated. 

   - **ii.** The member requires initial biologic therapy due to involvement of high-risk joints (e.g., cervical spine, wrist, or hip), high disease activity, and/or those judged by their physician to be at high risk of disabling joint damage.

- **c.** If the request is for a non-preferred adalimumab product, the member has experienced therapeutic failure or intolerance to two (2) preferred adalimumab products.
